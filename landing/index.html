How a round works

No swaps, no waiting. Draft, commit, watch both books race, and the contract pays the winner, all in one minute.

1

Draft blind

Pick 3 of 5 assets, weight them, and choose long or short on each. Stake your USDG.

2

Commit-reveal

Your hand and the Boss's lock at the same instant. It counter-drafts blind, it can never copy you.

3

Race live

Both hands flip face-up the second the round starts. Watch the two books climb to the bell.

4

Settle on-chain

Best return wins the USDG pot, settled in a single transaction. Least loss wins if both are red.

Two ways to fight

How to play

Jump in from the browser with a wallet, or play hands-free from Telegram. Same battles, same on-chain leaderboard.

🌐

Play on the Web

MetaMask · plays in your browser at /play
  1. Open the web app and click Connect wallet (MetaMask).
  2. Approve the Robinhood Chain testnet network when prompted (details below).
  3. Grab a little test ETH for gas and USDG to stake from the faucet.
  4. Pick 3 assets, weight them to 100%, and toggle each long or short.
  5. Set your stake and hit STEP INTO THE PIT, the Boss joins automatically.
  6. Reveal, then watch both books race live to the bell. Winner takes the pot.

Play on Telegram

@LivePitBot, custodial, no wallet setup
  1. Open @LivePitBot and press Start. A play wallet is created for you.
  2. Choose your arena with /mode, stocks or crypto.
  3. Draft with /draft, using -TICKER to short an asset.
  4. Open the round with /battle; the Boss counter-drafts on its own.
  5. Track it with /status, the bot auto-reveals and alerts you on settle.
  6. Check the running score any time with /score.
